Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Fractional CMO?

A Fractional CMO provides strategic marketing leadership on a part-time or project basis, offering the expertise of a full-time CMO without the full-time commitment and cost. Perfect for growing companies that need strategic direction but aren't ready for a full-time executive.

How long do typical engagements last?

Engagements typically range from 3-12 months, depending on your goals and needs. Some clients work with me for ongoing strategic guidance, while others engage for specific projects or initiatives. There's no long-term commitment required.

What industries do you work with?

I've worked across various industries including SaaS, e-commerce, healthcare, fintech, and professional services. My expertise in SEO and technical marketing is applicable across all digital-first businesses.

Do you work with international companies?

Yes, I work with companies globally. Most of my work is done remotely, and I'm experienced in managing international marketing strategies and cross-cultural communication.

What's included in your services?

Services include strategic planning, team leadership, SEO optimization, analytics and reporting, process improvement, and stakeholder management. Each engagement is customized to your specific needs and goals.
